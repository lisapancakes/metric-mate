MetricMate

A lightweight internal tool to track project goals, progress, and outcomes from kickoff to final review.

What this is

MetricMate helps PMs and designers capture project goals, track progress at midterm, and summarize outcomes at the end of a project. It keeps everything in one place so you can quickly see:

  • What we set out to do
  • What actually happened
  • What we learned and what to do next

How it works

1) Kickoff – Set goals & context

Capture the project summary, business & product goals, user goals, and key pain points. This becomes the backbone of the dashboard.

2) Midterm – Check health & momentum

Update goal status, add risks, wins, learnings, and next steps. MetricMate updates the dashboard so everyone sees where things really stand.

3) Final – Summarize outcomes

Log final results, decisions, and what shipped. The final dashboard rolls everything up and can prefill a case-study form for you.

What MetricMate gives you

  • A single project dashboard that merges kickoff, midterm, and final data
  • Clear view of completed goals, addressed pain points, and open challenges
  • A prefilled case-study Google Form to turn real work into portfolio / marketing content faster
